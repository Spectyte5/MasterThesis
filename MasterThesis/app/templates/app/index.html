Wave Dispersion in Django

Ultrasonic wave have found various engineering applications. The major problem in these investigations is the fact that many ultrasonic waves are dispersive i.e., the relevant wave speed depends on the frequency of propagating waves. This numerical library provides dispersion characteristics for different wave types and boundaries.
